Our Dear Dead Drug Lord played its final performance on Sunday, January 5, 2020, but its spirit lives on.

“Four top-notch performances and a memorable debut from a playwright whose risk-taking talent is something to keep an eye on. Our Dear Dead Drug Lord should make your list of plays to see this season.”

Theatermania

In this fierce and feverish comedy, a gang of teenage girls gathers in an abandoned tree house to summon the ghost of Pablo Escobar. Are they messing with the actual spirit of the infamous cartel kingpin? Or are they really just messing with each other? A roller coaster ride through the danger and damage of girlhood – the teenage wasteland has never been so much twisted fun.

For the world premiere production from WP Theater and Second Stage, breakout playwright Alexis Scheer joined forces with director Whitney White and a cast that included Carmen Berkeley, Alyssa May Gold, Rebecca Jimenez and Malika Samuel.

“Our Dear Dead Drug Lord isn't for the faint of heart, but neither is coming of age.”
